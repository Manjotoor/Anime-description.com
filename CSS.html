Difference between Cartoon, Manga and Anime

Cartoons: There is no strict storyline. Cartoons are only aimed towards kids and there are no mature content. Anime: It is animated type cartoons. It has a storyline, different genres and mostly aimed at young adults. Manga: It is nothing but anime in a sketch. Most animes are mangas that have been converted into animated series for viewers.
